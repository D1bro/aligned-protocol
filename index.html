The Aligned Audit

The Aligned Audit helps you get honest about where you are, clear on who you are becoming, and focused on the first move that will create real change.

You can move through this process in your own time, with guided videos, or with the support of a coach. The breakthrough often begins when you stop guessing, face your reality honestly, and give yourself permission to change.

Before you begin
Score where you genuinely are today — not where you think you should be. An honest score is the most valuable thing you can give yourself right now.
Scoring Guide
1–2
Deeply out of alignment
3–4
Struggling / neglected
5–6
Inconsistent / needs attention
7–8
Strong but not fully aligned
9–10
Fully aligned / thriving

You will score each area honestly, then reflect on what is working, what is not working, and what would move it forward by one point.

1.Rate yourself from 1–10
2.Explain why you gave that score
3.What is currently working well — optional
4.What is not working well — optional
5.Name one move that would raise this area by one point
